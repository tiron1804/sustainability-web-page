Sustainability Tips

Practical, actionable advice for sustainable living on campus and in Aalen

🏠 Sustainable Dorm Living

Make your dorm room eco-friendly and energy-efficient

💡

Smart Energy Usage

Easy

Simple changes to reduce your energy consumption in the dorm.

  • Use LED bulbs (available at campus store)
  • Unplug electronics when not in use
  • Use power strips with switches
  • Set heating to 18-20°C maximum
💰 Potential Savings:
€15-25 per month on electricity
🌊

Water Conservation

Easy

Reduce water waste with these simple dorm hacks.

  • Take shorter showers (5 minutes max)
  • Fix leaky faucets immediately
  • Use a water-saving showerhead
  • Turn off tap while brushing teeth
🌍 Environmental Impact:
Save up to 2,000L water per month
♻️

Dorm Room Recycling System

Medium

Set up an efficient recycling system in your small space.

  • Use small bins for different materials
  • Learn Aalen's recycling guidelines
  • Coordinate with roommates
  • Weekly trips to campus recycling points
📊 Recycling Rate:
Increase from 30% to 80%
🌱

Indoor Plants & Air Quality

Easy

Improve air quality and wellbeing with dorm-friendly plants.

  • Choose low-maintenance plants (snake plant, pothos)
  • Use natural light from windows
  • Water with used pasta/rice water
  • Share plant care with roommates
🌿 Air Quality:
15-20% improvement in indoor air

📚 Sustainable Study Habits

Eco-friendly approaches to academic success

📱

Digital-First Studying

Easy

Reduce paper usage with digital study methods.

  • Use tablets/laptops for note-taking
  • Download digital textbooks when possible
  • Use cloud storage for organization
  • Share digital notes with classmates
🌳 Paper Saved:
500+ sheets per semester
🔄

Sustainable Printing

Medium

When printing is necessary, do it sustainably.

  • Print double-sided always
  • Use draft mode for internal documents
  • Reuse single-sided paper
  • Form printing groups with classmates
📉 Paper Reduction:
50% less paper usage
📖

Library & Resource Sharing

Easy

Maximize shared resources and reduce individual consumption.

  • Use library books instead of buying
  • Participate in textbook exchanges
  • Study in groups to share materials
  • Donate books after graduation
💰 Cost Savings:
€200-400 per semester

🍽️ Sustainable Food & Dining

Eat well while reducing your environmental footprint

🥗

Plant-Based Meals

Medium

Incorporate more plant-based options into your diet.

  • Try "Meatless Monday" at campus cafeteria
  • Cook vegetarian meals 3x per week
  • Explore local vegetarian restaurants
  • Join campus vegetarian cooking groups
🌍 CO₂ Reduction:
1.5 tons CO₂ per year
🥡

Zero-Waste Dining

Easy

Reduce packaging waste from food purchases.

  • Bring reusable containers to cafeteria
  • Use refillable water bottles
  • Avoid single-use utensils
  • Shop with reusable bags
🗑️ Waste Reduction:
90% less food packaging waste
🏪

Local & Seasonal Shopping

Medium

Support local farmers and reduce transport emissions.

  • Shop at Aalen weekly market (Saturdays)
  • Choose seasonal produce
  • Buy from local farmers
  • Reduce imported/packaged foods
🚛 Transport Reduction:
75% less food miles

💡 Quick Daily Tips

Morning: Unplug chargers after use - they consume energy even when not charging
Midday: Choose stairs over elevator when possible - save energy and stay fit
Evening: Turn off lights when leaving rooms - even LED bulbs add up
